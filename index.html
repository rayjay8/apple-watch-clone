Double Tap

Gestures make Apple Watch even easier to use
anytime, especially when your hands are full.
Just double tap your index finger and thumb
together to answer a call, open a notification,
play and pause music, and more.

2x brighter display

A brilliant
breakthrough.

The advanced display system in Apple Watch
Series 9 boosts the maximum brightness up to
2,000 nits — double Apple Watch Series 8 —
so it’s easier to read in full sun. It’s also better
in low‑light situations, like a movie theatre,
because it dims to just 1 nit.

HomePod control. Watch takes the stage. A second-generation

When music is playing, Apple Watch Series 9 opens the Now

on HomePod.
